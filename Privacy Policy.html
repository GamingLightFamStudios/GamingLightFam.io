1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Account Information: This includes your Roblox username, any information you provide in our games (such as in-game name or profile data), and your Roblox account information when interacting with our services.
  • Gameplay Data: We gather data about how you play our games. This may include your progress, achievements, in-game actions, and the items you purchase (such as game passes, Robux transactions, etc.).
  • Device Information: When you play our games, we may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies or similar technologies to track your preferences, improve your gaming experience, and analyze the performance of our game.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes:

  • Improving Game Experience: To better design, develop, and enhance our games, including fixing bugs, adding new features, and improving gameplay.
  • Personalization: To personalize your experience within our games based on your preferences, previous actions, and achievements.
  • Transaction Processing: If you make any in-game purchases, such as buying game passes or virtual items, we process these transactions through Roblox's platform. We use this information to complete your purchase and ensure a smooth transaction.
  • Communication: If you’ve opted in to receive notifications, we may contact you with updates about our games, new features, or special promotions. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.

3. Sharing Your Information

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information will not be shared with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share some data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the game or improve our services. These providers are required to keep your information confidential and secure.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the safety of others, comply with legal obligations, or enforce our Terms of Use.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your Roblox account and any activities associated with it. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact Roblox support immediately.

Please note that all in-game purchases are non-refundable, and you are responsible for the costs associated with any purchases made within our games

5. Data Security

We take steps to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure, including using encryption and secure protocols for data transmission. However, no system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: If any of your information is inaccurate or outdated, you have the right to request corrections.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of promotional communications at any time by following the instructions provided in our messages or by contacting us directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated policy will be posted on this page with the effective date. By continuing to play our games or interact with our services after changes are made, you agree to the updated terms.
